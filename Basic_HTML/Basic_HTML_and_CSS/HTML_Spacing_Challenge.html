Chevrolet Camaro

The Chevy Camaro is a mid-size American automobile manufactured by Chevrolet, classified as a pony car. It first went on sale on September 29, 1966, for the 1967 model year and was designed to compete with the Ford Mustang.

The Camaro was discontinued at the end of the 2024 model year with most powerful engine Chevy every built; the LS7©. Production ended in December 2023 at 11:59 p.m. However, Chevrolet has stated, "This is not the end of Camaro's story."
